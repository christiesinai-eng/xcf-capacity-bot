▶ How spend is calculated
Contracts don't carry a single "total value" — they carry a rate + a rate unit. Totals shown here are estimates, not invoiced amounts:
  • DAY rate → rate × business days (Mon–Fri) between contract start and end date.
  • HOUR rate → rate × business days × hours/day (8h for FULL_TIME, 4h for PART_TIME).
  • PROJECT / MILESTONE rate → used as-is (already a total, not multiplied).
Confirmed spend totals (in the cards and breakdown tables) only include contracts in ACCEPTED or COMPLETED state — draft, offered and revoked contracts are excluded from spend but still counted in contract totals. Figures are shown per currency, not converted — this sheet has contracts in AUD, GBP, USD and NZD with no FX conversion applied.
